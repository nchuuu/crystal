Personal Crystal
You mysteriously end up in a magical forest and stumble upon a toad who is being attacked by some dark force. In order to save the toad, you must either drink a potion that will turn yourself into a dark force to be able to combat it and save that toad. In this situation, you would be removed from the human realm permanently and stay a dark force unless you kill another innocent toad. The other option is to leave and let the toad be killed as you keep the guilt inside you while the toad simply watches you walk away, and once you exit the magical forest, you yourself would be turned into a toad, only able to present yourself as a human being when you are in the forest.


The grass, trees, animals, and all entities of the forest slowly disintegrate into sand. The once cool shaded realm full of life, is now a dry lifeless desert with the hot sun beating down on you. The previous curses have been lifted. However, you are now extremely exhausted after making your decision. All you can see are miles and miles of sand and no people. One option is to start walking in some random direction in hopes of finding some sign of life or someone to help you, with the possibility of finding nothing and becoming dehydrated. The other option is to stay put, conserve your energy, and hope that someone else finds you or wait until you have enough energy to continue on.


Wherever you are in your journey, the ground starts shaking. A white fountain emerges from the sand and the water is crystal blue. Drinking the water will not replenish you, however, drinking it would create a rainstorm in this desert, where the rain would satisfy your thirst and cool you down enough to be able to go on to try to find help, as well as explore the dessert. Another option is to dip your feet in the water which would create a glowing path of footprints that would lead you out of the desert.


The water from the fountain starts spewing out massive amounts of water. It fills the desert up like an ocean. The brim of the water seems to reach the universe, and it seems like you are floating mid air since you can breathe just fine. Alone in this universe, it’s important to reflect on your life. Do you choose to think about all the past memories you’ve had and miss? Things you regret doing or not doing? Or do you choose to think about the future and all the things you haven’t done yet and want to do? Wondering what is out there for you to achieve?


Drowning in your thoughts, you sink to the bottom and a door awaits you. The door does not budge, it’s locked, but your intuition tells you that a special crystal is inside.You can either take your time and go search for the key, or try to break open the door due to your eagerness.
